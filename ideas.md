### Approach 1: Professional Technical Authority

**Design Movement:** Corporate Modernism with Technical Precision

**Core Principles:**

- Structured hierarchy with clear visual separation between service categories
- Technical precision reflected in geometric layouts and grid systems
- Trust-building through professional aesthetics and clean typography
- Dark navy foundation emphasizing stability and expertise

**Color Philosophy:**

- **Primary:** Navy Blue (#001B44) - conveys technical expertise, stability, and professionalism
- **Accent:** Golden Yellow (#FFC107) - highlights CTAs and key information, suggesting precision and quality
- **Supporting:** Charcoal (#2C3E50) for secondary text, Light Gray (#F5F5F5) for backgrounds
- **Emotional Intent:** Trustworthy, competent, detail-oriented—the kind of technician you want in your home

**Layout Paradigm:**

- Asymmetric card-based system with left-aligned content blocks
- Service sections arranged in alternating left-right pattern to create visual rhythm
- Hero section features diagonal cut dividers separating sections
- Grid-based portfolio with hover zoom effects

**Signature Elements:**

1. Geometric accent bars (navy and gold) framing key sections
2. Technical icons (circuit patterns, electrical symbols) integrated into backgrounds
3. Diagonal dividers between sections creating forward momentum

**Interaction Philosophy:**

- Smooth hover states on service cards revealing additional details
- CTA buttons with subtle glow effects on hover
- Scroll-triggered animations revealing portfolio items
- WhatsApp button with pulse animation for urgency

**Animation:**

- Fade-in animations on section scroll (200ms ease-out)
- Hover scale effect (1.02x) on service cards
- Subtle pulse animation on WhatsApp CTA button
- Staggered entrance of portfolio items on page load

**Typography System:**

- **Headlines:** Poppins Bold (700) for main titles, Poppins SemiBold (600) for section headers
- **Body:** Poppins Regular (400) for descriptions, Poppins Medium (500) for emphasis
- **Hierarchy:** Large 48px headlines, 24px section headers, 16px body text

## Selected Design Direction

**I am proceeding with Approach 1: Professional Technical Authority**

This design philosophy best serves DAENGLISTRIK.ID's business goals:

- **Trust-building:** Navy and gold color scheme conveys expertise and reliability—critical for a service business
- **Lead generation focus:** Clear visual hierarchy guides visitors toward WhatsApp CTAs naturally
- **Service clarity:** Structured card-based layout makes it easy for customers to understand available services
- **Mobile optimization:** Geometric layout translates well to mobile screens with proper responsive adjustments
- **Local market positioning:** Professional aesthetic appeals to homeowners, business owners, and institutional clients in Makassar

### Design System Implementation

**Color Palette:**

- Navy Blue: #001B44 (primary, backgrounds, text)
- Golden Yellow: #FFC107 (CTAs, accents, highlights)
- Charcoal: #2C3E50 (secondary text, borders)
- Light Gray: #F5F5F5 (section backgrounds, cards)
- White: #FFFFFF (main background)

**Typography:**

- Font Family: Poppins (all weights)
- Headlines: Poppins Bold (700)
- Section Headers: Poppins SemiBold (600)
- Body: Poppins Regular (400)
- Emphasis: Poppins Medium (500)

**Key Visual Elements:**

- Geometric navy and gold accent bars
- Diagonal section dividers
- Technical icon integration
- Card-based service layout
- Smooth hover animations

**Interaction Design:**

- Hover scale effects on interactive elements
- Pulse animation on WhatsApp CTA
- Scroll-triggered section reveals
- Smooth color transitions

This direction positions DAENGLISTRIK.ID as a professional, trustworthy service provider while maintaining an approachable, modern aesthetic that converts visitors into leads.
